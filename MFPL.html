MFPL APP

Login/Signup

The login/signup page serves as the initial access point for users of the MFPL app. It provides a secure and personalized experience, allowing authorized individuals to access the app's features and settings

Screen Navigation

Screen Navigation The sidebar in the MFPL app serves as a convenient tool for screen navigation. It allows users to toggle between different screens and sections of the app seamlessly. By clicking on the respective icons in the sidebar, users can easily switch to the desired screen, such as the dashboard, PPM guidelines, feedback, controls, and info. This intuitive navigation feature enhances user experience, enabling quick access to different functionalities and information within the app.

Dashboard

The dashboard section of the app provides an overview of the wheelchair's performance. It displays critical information, such as battery life, patient weight, and lifting capacity. The dashboard section also provides real-time feedback on the wheelchair's status, such as whether the motors are running and whether the patient is being lifted. This feature allows healthcare providers to monitor the wheelchair's performance and make adjustments as needed.

PPM Guidelines

The PPM guidelines section provides instructions on how to maintain and operate the wheelchair properly. This feature is crucial because the wheelchair is a complex device that requires regular maintenance to function correctly. The guidelines provide step-by-step instructions on how to clean and maintain the wheelchair, as well as how to troubleshoot common problems. This feature ensures that the wheelchair operates optimally and prolongs its lifespan.

Feedback

The feedback section allows users to provide comments and suggestions on the wheelchair's functionality. Users can submit feedback directly from the app, and the information is sent to the development team for analysis. This feature allows the development team to improve the wheelchair's design and functionality based on user feedback.

Controls

The controls section is a critical feature of the app that enables the user to control the wheelchair's lifting mechanism. The icons used in the sidebar are clear and easy to understand, making it simple for the user to operate the wheelchair. The integration of the controls section with the motors ensures that the patient is lifted smoothly and safely.
