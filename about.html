MFPL is a medical lifting and transferring device designed to assist patients who are seriously crippled and dependent on caregivers or nurses for their daily tasks. IR Proximity Sensor for Blind Patients (2021)

This project is about the efficiency of the IR sensors. It will not give us rough signals to buzzer if the object crosses the sensor. It tracks the object, if the object is moved within 100-500 microseconds, the buzzer will not give us signals. But if it is a stable object, it gives a signal. If the blind is also a deaf, nothing to worry, there is a vibration actuator to make vibrations and give signals.
